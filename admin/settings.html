How this works

Pinterest doesn't push new pins to other apps in real time, so this connects to your account via Pinterest's official login, then pulls the latest pins from the board you choose. It syncs automatically every 30 minutes, or you can press "Sync Pinterest now" on the Drafts page any time. Each new pin appears as a draft — nothing goes live on the site until you add an affiliate link and publish it.
